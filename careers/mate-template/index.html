Why Work With Us?

Work On Something That Matters

We believe that the future of the internet should be decentralised and open, that previously siloed communications platforms should be interoperable and that individuals should control their own data and choose who they share it with.

Work in the open

Almost all of the software written at New Vector is open source. Everything you do will be out in the open, you will be supported not only by your colleagues, but also by a huge community just as passionate about the project as you are.

Work on something genuinely interesting

We are building massively scalable decentralised infrastructure, that can be deployed in a distributed manner across 1000s of servers, or equally be deployed to a single Raspberry Pi.

Work with everyone

Our products are for everyone and we know we cannot succeed without a diverse team. Our hiring process is designed to be humane and give candidates the best possible chance to show us what they can do. If we ever fall down on this, please let us know.

Work in a culture to be proud of

People tend to stay with the company for a long time, we take this a sign that we have a cohesive supportive culture, that we have engaging challenging work and that people can develop their skills and careers here for the long term.

FAQ

What is Matrix.org?

Matrix is an open standard for secure decentralised communication. It is made up of a spec, and two reference implementations Synapse and Dendrite

What is Riot.im?

Riot is the flagship client used to access the Matrix ecosystem. With its decentralised architecture and end-to-end encryption, Riot.im makes collaboration truly global, open, and trusted.

Who are New Vector?

New Vector is a commercial entity that employs the majority of the matrix.org and riot.im core team. New Vector exists to grow the Matrix ecosystem.

How does New Vector make money?

New Vector makes money through hosting services and consultancy

Where are New Vector's offices?

New Vector's head office is located in London UK, we also have an office in Rennes Fr

How can I learn more about working at New Vector?

Since almost everything we do is open source you can get a good feel for exactly what it would be like to work on these projects. Similarly joining dev-centric channels via Riot.im such as #matrix-dev:matrix.org provides a window into the core team and the community. Come and say hi!
